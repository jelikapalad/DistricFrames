District Frames is a team that has passionate individuals and is dedicated to this kind of career. District Frames’ founder, Nicko Buenaventura, has over ten years of experience in the video production industry. He began freelancing in his early years before deciding to create his own studio in 2014, at the age of 24. His work is known for its distinct aesthetic, ability to capture candid shots. The concept of his service is to provide quality videos for a reasonable price.
